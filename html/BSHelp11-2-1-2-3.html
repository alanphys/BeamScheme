Inflection Point Field SizeÂ¶

The field size (FS) is defined as the distance from the left Inflection Point Field Edge to the right Inflection Point Field Edge:

\[FS = d(RE) - d(LE)\]

Where d(RE) and d(LE) the linear distances from the profile centre to the right and left field edges respectively.

The profile must be centered on the origin as defined by the array or imaging modality for best results.
