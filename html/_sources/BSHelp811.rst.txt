Offset
======

Moves the profile away from the centre of the detector or image. Values can be negative or positive. Values can be incremented or decremented via the up and down arrows in the spin box. Values can be entered manually via keyboard.

|Hint| Hint: You can change the offset quickly by clicking with the mouse on the image.
