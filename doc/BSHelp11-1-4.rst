Dose Point Parameters
=====================

The traditional flatness parameters have no meaning for FFF beams. Therefore the dose at various points along the profile are used to describe the shape of the profile. The dose values at 20, 50, 60 and 80% of the distance from the origin as defined by the array or imaging modality to the edge of the field defined by the inflection point can be reported.
